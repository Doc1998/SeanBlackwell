About Sean

Meet Sean Blackwell

Seán Blackwell is a professional photography based in the southwest of Ireland, specialising in weddings, portraits, and personal events. His work blends natural light with creamy colours for a distinct and professional composition.

Whether capturing quiet moments in nature or the joy of human connection, Seán's goal is to create photographs that tell stories - honest, vibrant, and timeless.

“I find real joy in capturing those natural moments that can easily pass unnoticed, but in hindsight become timeless memories”

Sean Blackwell Photographer
