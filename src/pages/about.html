About Sean

Meet Sean Blackwell

Sean Blackwell is a professional photographer based in the Pacific Northwest, specializing in portraits, weddings, and landscapes. His work blends natural light with an eye for authentic emotion and artistic composition.

Whether capturing quiet moments in nature or the joy of human connection, Sean’s goal is to create photographs that tell stories — honest, vibrant, and timeless.

“Every photograph is an invitation to pause, reflect, and remember the beauty in ordinary moments.”

Sean Blackwell Photographer & Founder
